# Salmon River Water Quality Portal

### Jesse J Lamb & Aimee H Fullerton

jesse.j.lamb\@noaa.gov, aimee.fullerton\@noaa.gov

This repository includes data and scripts used to display patterns in water quality data collected from 15 sites in headwater streams of the Salmon River basin, Idaho. Empirical observations of water quality were collected by NOAA's Northwest Fisheries Science Center through the BPA-funded project 1991-028-00 'Monitoring the Migrations of Wild Snake River Spring/Summer Chinook Salmon Smolts'. Data from 1993-1997 were provided by the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory. Daily data include hourly stream temperatures (degrees Celsius) and water depths ("Level Surface Elevation" in feet).
